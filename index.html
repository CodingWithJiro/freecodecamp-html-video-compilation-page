Greatest Hits of Pop-punk Band blink-182

Pop-punk band "blink-182" is one of the most successful bands that etched a new generation of music for music enthusiasts. This site will showcase their songs that got them to fame and stardom!

All the Small Things

This song was released in 1999 by blink-182 as a second single from their third album called Enema of the State. Written by the band's vocalist/guitarist Tome DeLonge which was an ode to his then-girlfriend. This song one of their signature songs.

I Miss You

This song was released on February 2, 2004 from their album "2003". Written by band guitarist and vocalist Tom DeLonge bassist Mark Hoppus. The song speaks more on past relationships that had ended and the person longing to feel that love again.

What's My Age Again?

This song was part of the album "Enema of the State" which was released in April 1999. Both written by band vocalist and guitarist, Tom DeLonge, and bassist, Mark Hoppus. It features the coming of age for young adults but still acting immature and confused on their future.
